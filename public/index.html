About ME

My name is Ryan Murray. I am currently a Senior at Innovation Academy Charter School (IACS). My passion is soccer, and I played on the Varisty soccer team for all 4 years. My Senior year I was elected as one of the Captains on the team. After I graduate from IACS in June, I plan to attend college where I hope to merge my passion for soccer and soccer statistics into a gainful career within sports media.


One of my favorite soccer plays is to take corner kicks for the team. Below is a video of me taking a corner kick during our Senior night game against Lowell Catholic High School.
